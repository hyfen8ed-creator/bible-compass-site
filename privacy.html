Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 14, 2026

Overview

Bible Compass is a faith-based reflection app. We respect your privacy.

Data Collection

Bible Compass does not require accounts and does not collect personal information such as names, emails, locations, contacts, photos, or health data.

Users may voluntarily enter text for reflection. Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve app performance.

AI Usage

Bible Compass uses artificial intelligence to generate scripture-based reflections. AI responses are not used to identify users and are not stored as personal profiles.
